About Me

The story behind Pang English Studio.

Hi! I'm Pang.

Before starting my master's degree, I began teaching English to children and slowly discovered how much I genuinely enjoyed working with young learners. What originally started as something temporary gradually became something I became truly passionate about.

Over time, I realized that every child learns differently. Some students enjoy stories, some learn better through visuals, and many become more engaged when learning feels playful and interactive rather than stressful.

One thing I noticed very quickly is that children often learn much better when they are having fun.

That realization inspired me to start building this website. I wanted to create a space where English learning feels more interactive, colorful, and enjoyable for kids — not just worksheets and memorization, but activities that encourage children to actively use the language and feel more confident while learning.

Many of my students especially enjoy learning through games such as memory games, matching activities, and small interactive exercises. However, I also encourage them to say the words out loud while playing, because speaking and repeating vocabulary helps children remember and use English more naturally and confidently.

A lot of the activities and ideas on this website were inspired by my own students. Since every child has different interests, personalities, and learning styles, I try to create different types of activities so that each learner can hopefully find something they enjoy.

This website is still growing, and I'm continuing to improve and develop it step by step alongside my teaching journey.

While I am not a formally certified teacher, this website is built from real teaching experience, continuous learning, and genuine care for helping children enjoy English more. I'm still learning and improving every day, so some materials may continue to evolve over time as I gain more experience and understanding.

My goal is to keep this resource free for students and parents who want extra English practice at home.

If you have any suggestions, feedback, notice anything that could be improved, or would like to collaborate in the future, please feel free to reach out. I'm always open to learning, improving, and finding better ways to make English learning more enjoyable for children. 🤍

Thank you for visiting, and I hope this little space can make learning English feel a bit more fun, comfortable, and exciting for your child.
